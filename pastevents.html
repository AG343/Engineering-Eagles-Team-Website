Clovis Remote Qualifier#1

For our first qualiifier in our first FTC season, we managed to bring out our robot, Jeff Potato to the competition with it earning us 4th place for Robot Performance. In addition to the robot, our process in building, programming, repeatedly optimizing it earned us the Design Award.


Our high score!

San Mateo Qualiifier#2

We significantly improved our robot since our first qualifier, designing a better intake and carousel. Our point average increased by over 15 points, going from 103 to 119. However, our high score managed to stay around the same with a 132, meaning the only thign that improved was consistency. We also started outreach, reaching out to industry professionals and communicating with BASIS in order to have a presentation to give the knowledge of FIRST and STEM to the community.
